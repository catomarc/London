Activities in London

Outdoor Activities

Enjoy a vast range of activities that London Outdoors has to offer this summer!
Explore the following: Outdoor films at Somerset House, Dining in the sky, Greenwich Peninsula, GO ape! and so much more

Indoor Activities

On a rainy day in London you might be looking for some fun Indoors such as Indoor crazy golf, Gaming brunch at the Platform, bottomless karaoke, roller blading clubs and so much more!
Looking for a thrill? Check out the London Dungeons for an exhilirating experience.

Family Activities

Take your family to Battersea Park Children's Zoo to learn about many animals or go on a journey across the Thames on Emirates Airline Cable car.
If your children love marine life take them to the Sea Life London Aquariam which houses over 500 species.

Entertainment and Learning Activities

Do the South Kensington Museum Triple, visit the Tate Modern or if you like plays visit Shakespeare's Globe Theatre.
Some beautiful ad historic buildings such as St. Pauls Cathedral and Westminster Abbey are highly recommended.
